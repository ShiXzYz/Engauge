## How It Works

- Students drag seeds from sidebar to plant in empty pots
- Each pot starts at **Stage 0** (empty)
- When planted, it becomes **Stage 1** (mound/seed)
- Students earn 10 XP per poll answer
- At 50 XP, the water can fills up
- Students click any planted pot to water it and grow it one stage
- Goal: All 9 plants blooming (Stage 5)!

## Image Specifications

- **Format**: PNG with transparency
- **Recommended size**: 120x120 pixels (or up to 200x200)
- **Style**: Pixel art recommended (CSS applies `image-rendering: pixelated`)
- **Important**: Each sprite **includes the terracotta pot** at the bottom
- **Background**: Transparent (blends with greenhouse floor)

## Color Palette Suggestions

- Terracotta Pot: `#CD853F`
- Dark Soil: `#3E2723`
- Sprout Green: `#6B8E23`
- Mature Green: `#228B22`
- Flower Colors: Pink `#FF69B4`, Yellow `#FFD700`, Purple `#9370DB`

## Fallback

If images are not found, the system will automatically display emoji placeholders until you add your custom PNGs.
